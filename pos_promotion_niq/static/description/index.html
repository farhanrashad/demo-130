Odoo POS Promotion

Enhance promotion program for Odoo POS

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo community edition but not tested in Enterprise version.

POS Promotion Key Features:

  • Set Up Promotion Base On Product
    • Discount % on product
    • Discount amount on product
    • Fixed price on product
    • Buy X Get Y Free
    • Buy X Get Y with Disc. %
    • Buy X Get Y with Disc. Amount
    • Buy X Get Y Fixed Price
    • Product Combo On Product
  • Setup Promotion Base On Template Product
    • Discount % on template
    • Discount amount on template
    • Fixed price on template
  • Setup Promotion Base On Category Product
    • Discount % on category
    • Discount amount on category
    • Fixed price on category
  • Setup Promotion Base On Combo Product
  • Setup Promotion Base On Total Amount of Order
    • Product - Discount % Base on Total Amount
    • Product - Fixed Price Base on Total Amount
    • Product - Get Free Base on Total Amount
    • Product - Discount Amount Base on Total Amount
    • Order - Discount % Total Amount
  • Import Promotion Product batch by Excel
  • Export Existing Promotion Product List to Excel
  • POS Summary Flexible Reports
    • Daily Sale By Shop
    • Today Sale By Shop
    • Today Sale By Session
